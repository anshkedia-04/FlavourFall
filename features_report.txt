Why FlavourFall should switch from Petpooja to ToastMaster
Executive Summary
FlavourFall operates 6 restaurants with a hierarchical team structure (Owner → Manager → Captain → Waiter / Barista / Chef) and currently runs Petpooja as its POS platform. While Petpooja is a capable commodity POS trusted by 1,00,000+ outlets, it is fundamentally a billing-first tool with multi-outlet support bolted on as add-ons.
ToastMaster, already a rich single-outlet café & bakery management system, has a significant head start in UX quality, recipe-linked inventory, and staff management. With targeted enterprise additions outlined in this report, ToastMaster can deliver:
A purpose-built multi-outlet command centre vs Petpooja's franchise module
Role-based access for every level of FlavourFall's hierarchy
True table & floor management that Petpooja lacks natively
Aggregator integration (Swiggy / Zomato) in a single screen
An offline-capable PWA vs Petpooja's dependency on connectivity
White-label branding for FlavourFall — not a generic SaaS logo
This report identifies 22 key feature additions across 8 modules, maps each gap directly against Petpooja's capabilities, and provides a prioritised development roadmap.

Section 2 — 22 New Features to Build
The following features are required to fully beat Petpooja for the FlavourFall use-case. They are grouped by module and prioritised by business impact.
Module A — Multi-Outlet Command Centre
#
Feature & Rationale
Business Impact
A1
Central Owner Dashboard
Single screen showing live revenue, order counts, low-stock alerts, and staff status across all 6 FlavourFall outlets simultaneously. Outlet selector with quick drill-down. Petpooja calls this 'Head-office module' but it lacks real-time visual quality.
Critical
A2
Outlet Switcher & Profile Management
Managers can manage their own outlet; the owner can switch between all 6. Each outlet stores its own menu, staff, and settings while sharing a master CRM and inventory catalogue.
Critical
A3
Cross-Outlet Analytics & Benchmarking
Compare KPIs between outlets — revenue, avg order value, table turnover, top items — with chart overlays. Identify which outlet is underperforming and why. Petpooja offers zone groupings; ToastMaster should offer visual benchmarking.
High
A4
Central Kitchen / Commissary Module
A 'hub' outlet that produces semi-finished goods (doughs, sauces, marinated proteins) and dispatches them to branch outlets. Branch KDS shows items sourced from central kitchen. Stock auto-deducted at the hub.
High
Module B — Table & Floor Management
#
Feature & Rationale
Business Impact
B1
Interactive Floor Plan Builder
Drag-and-drop visual floor map per outlet. Tables marked with live status colours: Available (green), Occupied (amber), Reserved (blue), Needs Cleaning (grey). Critical for fine-dining service at FlavourFall.
Critical
B2
Table Reservation & Booking System
Accept phone, walk-in, and online reservations. Show a calendar view of bookings. Send automated WhatsApp/SMS confirmation. Block tables in advance. Petpooja requires third-party integration for this.
High
B3
Split Bill & Merge Tables
Split a single table bill between multiple guests (equal or custom split). Merge bills from two tables into one — essential for group dining. Each split generates an individual receipt.
Critical
B4
Course-wise / KOT-wise Order Firing
Waiters can hold items and fire them to the kitchen in courses (starters → mains → desserts). Kitchen only sees the active course. Prevents food arriving all at once.
Medium
Module C — Role-Based Hierarchy & Captain App
#
Feature & Rationale
Business Impact
C1
5-Level Role Hierarchy
Owner → Manager → Captain → Waiter/Barista/Chef. Each level has specific permissions. Owner sees all 6 outlets. Manager sees one outlet fully. Captain takes table orders. Waiter/Chef has limited view. Matches FlavourFall's exact org structure.
Critical
C2
Captain Tablet App (Mobile POS)
Captains walk the floor with a tablet, take table orders, and send directly to KDS — no walking to billing counter. Real-time sync. Supports combo and add-on selection. This is Petpooja's 'Captain App' — ToastMaster must build this.
Critical
C3
Waiter Handoff & Table Assignment
Assign specific tables to specific waiters per shift. All orders on those tables are attributed to that waiter. Enables per-waiter performance tracking and tip allocation.
High
C4
Performance Scorecards per Staff
Weekly/monthly report per staff member: covers, avg order value, upsell rate, attendance %, customer ratings. Exportable. Gives managers data to reward or coach staff.
Medium
Module D — KOT & Kitchen Enhancements
#
Feature & Rationale
Business Impact
D1
Multi-Station KOT Printing
Each menu item is tagged to a kitchen station (Grill, Cold Kitchen, Bar, Bakery). On order, separate KOT slips auto-print at each station. Avoids mis-routing. Petpooja offers this natively — ToastMaster must match it.
Critical
D2
KOT Modification & Void Tracking
Any modification or cancellation of a fired KOT requires Manager/Captain authorisation with reason logging. Prevents kitchen fraud. Full audit trail.
High
Module E — Online Ordering & Aggregator Integration
#
Feature & Rationale
Business Impact
E1
Swiggy & Zomato Live Order Integration
Pull live orders from Swiggy and Zomato directly into ToastMaster's order queue. Auto-generate KOT. Manage aggregator-specific menus and toggle availability. This single feature is a major Petpooja strength — essential to match.
Critical

Upload Swiggy/Zomato settlement reports and auto-reconcile against ToastMaster's sales data. Shows commission charged, cancelled orders, net payout, and discrepancies. Petpooja offers this; it's a major pain point for chain operators.
High

Feature & Rationale
